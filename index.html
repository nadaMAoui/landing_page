About Fintella

At Fintella, we believe that everyone deserves access to reliable financial information and tools. Inspired by our own experiences in managing personal finances, we embarked on a journey to create a platform that simplifies financial management and empowers individuals to take control of their money.

This project is part of our portfolio for Holberton School, where we honed our skills in web development and applied our knowledge of finance to build Fintella. We are excited to share our creation with you and invite you to join us on this financial journey.
